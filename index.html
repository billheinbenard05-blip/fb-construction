What we do

Design services built for the build.

Every drawing we issue is made to survive contact with a construction site — coordinated, dimensioned, and priced.

Architectural Design

Concept to construction drawings for residential and commercial buildings — floor plans, elevations, sections, and 3D visualisations that capture exactly how a space will look and live.

  • Residential house plans
  • Commercial & mixed-use buildings
  • 3D renders & walkthroughs

Structural Design

Engineering that keeps the architecture standing — foundation design, reinforced concrete and steel detailing, load calculations, and full structural drawing sets for approval and site use.

  • Foundation & framing design
  • Reinforcement & steel detailing
  • Structural calculations & reports

Road Design

Geometric and pavement design for access roads, estate roads, and civil infrastructure — alignment, cross-sections, drainage, and earthworks quantities for accurate, buildable plans.

  • Road alignment & geometry
  • Drainage & earthworks design
  • Pavement specification

Bills of Quantities

Detailed, item-by-item BoQs for proposal bidding and project budgeting — measured to standard methods of measurement so your tender numbers hold up under scrutiny.

  • Tender & bidding documents
  • Material & cost estimation
  • Budget & variance tracking

How we work

From brief to bid-ready drawings.

A clear, sequential path — because every plan we hand over has to be buildable and every BoQ has to be defensible.

  1. 01

    Brief & site assessment

    We sit with you — in person or over a call — to understand the brief, the budget, and the site conditions before a single line is drawn.

  2. 02

    Concept design

    Initial layouts, elevations, and massing options. You review and refine the direction before we commit to detailed drawings.

  3. 03

    Structural & technical detailing

    Foundations, framing, and — where relevant — road geometry and drainage are engineered and drawn to construction standard.

  4. 04

    Bill of quantities

    Every drawing is measured and priced into a detailed BoQ, ready to go out for contractor bidding or bank financing.

  5. 05

    Handover & support

    You receive full drawing sets and documentation, plus our support through approvals, tendering, and into construction.
